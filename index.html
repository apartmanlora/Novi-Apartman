About apartment

Apartment Lora is a three-room apartment on the ground floor of a family house with a spacious yard located in a quiet part of Crikvenica. Parking for cars is provided in the yard of the apartment. The distance from the city center and the main square and the beach is only 15 minutes' walk. The apartment is located on an elevation, so you need less time when going to the city and the beach. In the immediate vicinity of the apartment there is also a newly arranged children's playground and guests benefit from complimentary WiFi and private parking available on site.

Beaches in Crikvenica: Town Beach 550 m, Crni Molo Beach 550 m, Beach International 650 m, Kastel Beach 750 m, Podvorska Dog Beach 1.000 m.
